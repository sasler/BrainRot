MINI GOLF 3D
A neon-lit 3D mini golf challenge built for quick, satisfying shots, smooth physics, and delightfully mean courses.

How to Play

Drag from the ball to pull back and release to shoot. The farther you pull, the more power you unleash.

Camera

Hold right-click and drag to orbit around the green. The camera follows the ball smoothly between shots.

Hazards

Water resets you to the tee with a penalty stroke. Ramps, slopes, walls, and bumpers all affect the roll.

Goal

Sink the ball in the cup in as few strokes as possible. Beat par across all four holes to finish with style.
